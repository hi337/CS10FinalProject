Why You Should Donate

Beyond just feeling great that you helped someone in need, you get a raffel ticket to win a prize!

Through donating your old prescription eyeglasses to CLERC (Lions Clubs International Eyeglass Recycling Centre) provides recycled eyewear free of charge to people in developing countries.

Glasses are crucial for everyone due to their significant impact on education, and therefore productivity and quailty of life. By providing glasses we can help people in developing countries overcome visual impairments, enabling them to read, learn, and fully participate in educational activities.
